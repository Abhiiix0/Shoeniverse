About us

Founder and co-founder started this company in 2010 because they saw that a lot of people wanted their own customised shoes but there were no such companies so we came up with this idea. From past 4 years we have been daily customising 50k + shoes across the country.we also have our branches in different states of our country including Maharashtra, gujrat, west bengal etc.Our mission and aim is to spread our work worldwide in next 2 years
